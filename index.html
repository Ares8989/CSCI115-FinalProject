Welcome to Gam3r Reviews: a website for gamers, by gamers.
Have you ever read a review on a website that reads as though the person never used the product? Have you ever read a review and wondered if the person writing it is a actual person and not a AI?
Here at Gam3r Reviews, we pledge that all of our reviews are written by actual people who actually enjoy video games. We aim to provide you with a in-depth, but unbaised, idea of whether or not a game is a good buy, or going to make you (or your wallet) cry.
